How I work

I come in calmly and pay attention. We look at how you sell, where the friction is, and what keeps you up at night. I review your current tools and numbers, then map out where things are breaking or double handling work.

We fix the foundation first. That might mean restructuring products, changing how payouts hit the books, or simplifying the way you track stock. I write things down in plain language so you and your team know what is happening and why.
