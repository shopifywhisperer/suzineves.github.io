Who I Work With
Owners whose back end is fighting them.
I’m best for owners who are in the business every day and feeling the weight of systems that don’t quite match reality. You’re juggling product, events, money movement, and constant decisions — and you want the back end to feel grounded so you can focus on the part you’re actually good at.
How I Work
Small, steady phases so you can keep moving.
We start with a walkthrough of what feels messy or heavy. I look at Shopify, your books, and the workflows around both to see what is really going on.

From there, we clean things up in small, steady phases so you can keep running the business while the systems get better. Once everything is synced, we shift into light ongoing support so it stays that way.
What I Do
The unglamorous pieces that keep things true.
I handle the pieces that connect your store, your bookkeeping, and your operations. That includes monthly bookkeeping support, syncing Shopify and QuickBooks so reports match reality, and untangling payouts, refunds, discounts, and money movement.

I also clean up products, variants, and collections so your store is easier to run — for you, not just for the software.
