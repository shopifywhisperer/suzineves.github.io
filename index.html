WHAT I HELP WITH

Most people reach out because something in the workflow isn’t adding up — Shopify shows one thing, the books show another, or the day-to-day process feels heavier than it should. It’s not chaos, it’s just work that isn’t flowing the way it needs to.

Here’s where I usually step in:

Products, collections, settings, and daily updates aligned with what’s happening right now — accurate, consistent, and easy to maintain.

Numbers that reflect the same reality

Payouts, refunds, discounts, fees, and deposits all lining up so the financial side and Shopify side match without extra work or second-guessing.

Operational issues that need a steady hand

The recurring glitches — mismatched data, duplicate behavior, processes that keep slowing things down. I sort through what’s happening and get it running the way it should.

HOW WORKING TOGETHER FEELS

You tell me what’s happening — the parts that feel off, the areas you keep circling, the things that aren’t matching what you expect. It can be brief. It doesn’t need to be organized.

I review the setup you already have: your Shopify configuration, your financial flow, how updates are managed, and where the inconsistencies are showing up. The goal is to understand the system as it is, so we can see what’s solid, what needs tightening, and what needs a fresh start.

From there, we make practical changes that hold — cleaning up the areas that create extra work, tightening the parts that drift, and getting the workflow to operate the way it should. It’s steady, detailed work that makes the day-to-day feel easier.

FIT / NOT FIT
A good fit:

You have an active business and the core operations are established, but Shopify, bookkeeping, and daily workflows need to be aligned so everything reflects the same reality. You want clear, consistent operations supported by someone who understands the details without adding complexity.

Not a fit:

This isn’t the right match if you need a custom Shopify theme built from the ground up, if the business requires urgent intervention, or if you’re looking for a full team or agency structure. My work is steady, detailed operational support — not surface-level shortcuts or full-scale rebuilds.
