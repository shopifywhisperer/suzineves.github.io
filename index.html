How I work

We start by walking through a normal week. How you sell, who touches what, and where things feel heavier than they should.

From there, we tackle the pieces that will make the biggest difference with the least chaos. That might mean restructuring products, changing how payouts land in the books, or simplifying how you track stock.

The goal is not a perfect diagram. The goal is something you can live with on a busy day.
