Who I work with

I am best for owners who are stretched thin and want the back end of the business to stop fighting them. You are in it every day, juggling product, events, money movement, and all the decisions in between. You want your systems to feel grounded so you can focus on the part you are actually good at.

How I work

We start with a walkthrough of what feels messy or heavy. I look at Shopify, your books, and the workflows around both, and map out what is really going on.

From there, we clean things up in small, steady phases so you can keep running the business while the systems get better. Once everything is stable, you choose whether you want me to stay on monthly or hand it back with clear notes and simple habits.

What I do

I handle the pieces that connect your store, your bookkeeping, and your operations. That includes monthly bookkeeping support, syncing Shopify and QuickBooks so reports match reality, and untangling payouts, refunds, discounts, and daily money movement.

I also clean up products, variants, and collections so your store matches how you actually sell, and put light structure around events, inventory, and daily tasks. The goal is simple: a business that feels lighter to run, with numbers you can trust.
